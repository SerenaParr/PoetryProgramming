POEM EXCHANGE

A few months ago I participated in a poem exchange via email in which I sent one poem out and received four in return. The text below separates out the stanzas of each of the five poems and jumbles them together.

The exercise was performed manually, as inspired by Dadaist poetry, the Cut-up Method and Lynn Cherny's work.
